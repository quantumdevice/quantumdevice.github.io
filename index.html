About Us
Hefei Quantum Precision Device Co. Ltd (QPD) is a high-tech enterprise which focuses on developing frontier quantum technologies. The business scope of QPD includes quantum computations, quantum metrology, and high performance instruments. QPD provides instruments including Electron Paramagnetic Resonance (ESR) Spectrometer, Optical Detection Magnetic Resonance (ODMR) Spectrometer and high performance electronic devices. These devices can be applied in areas such as physics, chemistry, biology, aerospace and medical devices.

Our Team
Dr. Xi Qin
Dr. Xi Qin is an associate researcher at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He was previously the director of a pre-research project and the chief designer of scientific satellite projects at the SECM (Shanghai Engineering Center for Microsatellites). He brings valuable knowledge in the realization of instruments and electronics, and in the development of control software. After joining QPD, his group have designed several products with leading technique indicators. In pursuit of the long-term development of QPD, Qin is also devoted to construction of the Innovative Quantum Instruments Platform, which serves as an open platform for investigating advanced quantum technologies and lowering the difficulties of frontier research.

Arbitrary Sequence  Generator
An arbitrary Sequence Generator (ASG-GT50) is an eight-channel no-dead-time pulse sequence generator. The pulse signal output by ASG-GT50 has a time resolution of 50 ps, and a dynamic range from 5 ns – 2 s, which surpasses the standard technical specifications among similar products from market. ASG-GT50 can be widely used in quantum control, quantum metrology, nuclear/electron spin magnetic resonance, high precision time delay, synchronous control system, high-speed clock distribution and other cutting-edge technologies. It is the latest released pulse/delay generator with discounts available now (for customers ordering multiple units).

任意序列发生器（型号ASG-GT50）是一款八通道无死时间脉冲序列发生器。该产品时间精度高达50皮秒，动态范围为5纳秒至2秒，这都远远超越了目前市面上所有其他相关产品。ASG-GT50可以被广泛的应用在量子控制、量子精密测量、核/顺磁共振谱学、高精度时延、同步系统、高速时钟分布以及相关的尖端科技应用。ASG-GT50是最新型号，目前正享有优惠政策（尤其对订购多台设备的客户）。
