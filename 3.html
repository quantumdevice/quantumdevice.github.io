Hefei Quantum Precision Device Co. Ltd @2017 Copyright
Hefei Quantum Precision Device Co. Ltd (QPD) is a high-tech enterprise which focuses on the revolution of the frontier quantum technology.  The business scope of QPD includes high fidelity quantum computations, high precision quantum metrology, and high performance quantum instruments. QPD provides quantum instruments including Electron Paramagnetic Resonance (ESR) Spectrometer, Optical Detection Magnetic Resonance (ODMR) Spectrometer and high performance electronics equipment.

CEO
QPD Co-founder and Chief Technologist Dr. Xi Qin is an associate researcher at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He was previously the director of a pre-research project and the chief designer of scientific satellite projects at the SECM (Shanghai Engineering Center for Microsatellites). After joining QPD, his group have designed several products with leading technique indicators. In pursuit of the long-term development of QPD, Qin is also devoted to construction of the Innovative Quantum Instruments Platform, which serves as an open platform for investigating advanced quantum technologies and lowering the difficulties of frontier research.
